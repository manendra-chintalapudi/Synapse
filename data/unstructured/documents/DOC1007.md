# Inspection Report — DOC1007
**Document type:** Inspection Report
**Subject:** coil C10172 / EQ-FUR-02
**Related deviation:** DEV1451  |  **Severity:** LOW
**Equipment:** EQ-FUR-02 — Electric Arc Furnace Line 2 (Electric Arc Furnace)

## 1. Scope of Inspection
This report documents a targeted inspection of coil C10172 (grade IS2062-E350, 11.58 mm × 1610.6 mm, heat H2026-0136) and its production equipment EQ-FUR-02 (Electric Arc Furnace Line 2). The inspection was triggered by deviation DEV1451 and aimed to
confirm the reported condition, assess its extent, and recommend disposition. It was carried out at
Jamshedpur Unit - Furnace Hall using visual examination and standard measurement practice.

## 2. Method
The inspection combined visual surface examination, dimensional checks where applicable, and a review of the associated
process and maintenance records for EQ-FUR-02. Findings were compared against the applicable acceptance
criteria for the product grade and the equipment's normal operating condition.

## 3. Findings
Surface bump / raised defect detected on coil. The condition was verified to be consistent with the raised deviation. The affected region was
characterised and photographed for the record. Surrounding material/equipment was checked for similar indications to
determine whether the issue is isolated or systematic. No additional unrelated defects were noted beyond those
described here.

## 4. Assessment
Based on the findings, the condition is classified as **LOW** severity, consistent with the
originating deviation. The likely origin is process variation on EQ-FUR-02
(Electric Arc Furnace Line 2); this is referred for root cause analysis where severity or recurrence warrants.

## 5. Recommended Actions
- Apply the disposition defined in deviation DEV1451 (re-inspection, rework/re-grade or hold as appropriate).
- Reinforce monitoring on EQ-FUR-02 and review the relevant operating procedure.
- Verify effectiveness at the next production run and close the deviation once confirmed.
