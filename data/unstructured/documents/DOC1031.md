# Standard Operating Procedure — DOC1031
**Title:** Hot Rolling Mill Preventive Maintenance Checklist
**Procedure ID:** PROC-020  |  **Applies to:** EQ-HRM-02 — Hot Rolling Mill Line 2 (Hot Rolling Mill)
**Location:** Jamshedpur Unit - Furnace Hall

## 1. Purpose
This procedure defines the standard method for the task titled "Hot Rolling Mill Preventive Maintenance Checklist" on the hot rolling mill. It exists
to ensure the activity is carried out safely, consistently and in a way that protects personnel, equipment and product
quality, and to provide a training and audit reference for operators and maintenance staff.

## 2. Scope
This SOP applies to all operators, maintenance technicians and supervisors who operate or work on EQ-HRM-02
(Hot Rolling Mill Line 2) at Jamshedpur Unit - Furnace Hall. It covers the normal execution of the task and the
associated safety controls. It does not replace equipment-specific manufacturer instructions or statutory requirements.

## 3. Responsibilities
The shift supervisor authorises the activity and confirms isolation where required. The assigned technician executes the
steps and records completion. The equipment engineer maintains this procedure and reviews it after any related incident.

## 4. Prerequisites & PPE
Confirm the correct permit-to-work is in place, required isolations are applied, and standard PPE plus any
task-specific protection (as noted below) is worn. Ensure tools, spares and measuring equipment are available and
calibrated before starting.

## 5. Procedure Steps
1. Perform the scheduled preventive maintenance on the hot rolling mill per the maintenance calendar.
2. Inspect bearings, seals, couplings and drive components for wear, and top up or replace lubricants as specified.
3. Check sensor and instrumentation calibration against reference values.
4. Close out the work order with parts used and observations.

## 6. Safety Notes
- High-temperature strip and rolls - keep clear of the roll gap and pinch points.
- Stored hydraulic energy - isolate and bleed to zero before intervention.
- Rotating spindles and couplings - guards must be in place before start.
- Stop work and escalate to the shift supervisor if any parameter falls outside the normal operating band
  (reference: Rolling temperature: 1050-1250 degC, Mill stand separating force: up to ~30 MN).

- Applicable BIS / ASTM quality standards and The Factories Act, 1948 safety provisions.
- Plant CMMS maintenance schedule for the hot rolling mill.
