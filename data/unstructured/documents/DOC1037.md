# Deviation Report — DOC1037
**Document type:** Deviation / Non-Conformance Report
**Related deviation:** DEV1148  |  **Severity:** MEDIUM
**Affected coil:** C10150 — grade IS1786-Fe500, 5.95 mm × 992.6 mm, 19856.2 kg (heat H2026-0076, produced 2026-03-03)
**Associated equipment:** EQ-BF-03 — Blast Furnace Line 3

## 1. Description of Deviation
Surface scratch defect identified on coil, non-conforming to visual acceptance. The non-conformance was identified during routine quality inspection and flagged in the QMS
against the affected material. The condition falls outside the visual and dimensional acceptance criteria applied at
this stage of production and was therefore raised as a formal deviation for disposition.

## 2. Standard / Specification Breached
The deviation is assessed against BIS IS:1786 (High Strength Deformed Steel Bars) [STD-IS1786-01] surface-quality / acceptance requirements. The relevant clauses cover surface finish, freedom from injurious defects,
and conformance of the delivered product to its declared grade. Because the observed condition compromises conformance,
the material cannot be released against the standard without disposition.

## 3. Severity Assessment
This deviation is classified as **MEDIUM** severity. Classification reflects the defect type, its likely effect
on downstream performance and the frequency with which this defect class is observed. Rarer and structurally
significant defects are escalated to higher severity to ensure appropriate engineering review.

## 4. Root Cause (Preliminary)
A preliminary review points to process variation during production on EQ-BF-03
(Blast Furnace Line 3). A full root cause analysis is initiated where the severity or recurrence
of the defect warrants it, linking this deviation to any related equipment failures or maintenance events.

## 5. Disposition
**Decision:** Segregate and rework/re-grade; re-test before release.
Material is held in the QMS with this deviation reference until the disposition is completed and verified. Any rework
is re-inspected and re-tested against the applicable standard before the hold is released.
