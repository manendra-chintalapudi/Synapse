# Equipment Manual — DOC1065
**Equipment:** EQ-ROT-01 — Runout Table Cooling System #1
**Type:** Runout Table  |  **Location:** Rajendra Steel Plant, Mumbai Unit - Rolling Mill  |  **Commissioned:** 2021-03-08
**Preventive-maintenance interval:** quarterly

## 1. Overview
The Runout Table Cooling System #1 (EQ-ROT-01) is the controlled-cooling table that cools the strip from finishing-delivery to coiling temperature. It is installed at Rajendra Steel Plant, Mumbai Unit - Rolling Mill and was
commissioned on 2021-03-08. It is a critical asset in the plant's production flow and is managed under the
CMMS reliability programme, with a formal preventive-maintenance interval of **quarterly**. This manual excerpt
summarises the machine's key specifications, normal operating envelope, common failure modes and maintenance and safety
requirements. It should be read together with the original equipment manufacturer (OEM) documentation and the relevant
standard operating procedures; the values quoted here are indicative, industry-typical figures for a machine of this
class rather than exact OEM design data.

## 2. Key Specifications
- **cooling type**: laminar + ultra-fast cooling
- **table length m**: 120
- **header banks**: 20
- **finishing delivery temp C**: 880
- **coiling temp C**: 600

## 3. Operating Parameters
The equipment must be operated within its qualified envelope; representative set-points include cooling type = laminar + ultra-fast cooling, table length m = 120, header banks = 20. Operators
log the principal parameters each shift and compare them against these limits. Any sustained excursion outside the
normal band — particularly on temperature, pressure, tension, cooling-water flow or drive load as applicable to this
machine — must be raised through the CMMS and, where product or safety is affected, escalated for root cause analysis.
Start-up and shutdown follow the dedicated SOP, bringing the machine to condition gradually while watching for abnormal
vibration, noise or thermal signatures.

## 4. Common Failure Modes
The following failure modes are characteristic of this machine type and drive its inspection and spares strategy:
- **roller bearing seizure**
- **header nozzle clogging**
- **cooling water valve fault**
- **roller drive motor fault**
- **scale build-up**

Early-warning indicators for these modes (temperature drift, abnormal vibration, tension/level instability, water-flow
loss) are trended in the condition-monitoring system so that developing faults are caught before they cause an
unplanned outage or a quality deviation on the product.

## 6. Safety Warnings
- Large volumes of cooling water and steam — slip and scald hazards along the table.
- Hot strip on driven rollers — pinch points; lockout before clearing scale or jams.
- Roller drive motors and couplings — isolate before maintenance.
- Only trained, authorised personnel may operate or maintain this equipment, under the plant permit-to-work system and
  in compliance with The Factories Act, 1948 safety provisions.
