The root cause analysis RCA1008, performed by Suresh Malhotra (equipment engineer, Shift C), concluded: Overhead Crane (Rolling Mill Bay) #1 flagged an overstrain on the main hoist: a lift near rated capacity combined with a shock (snatch) load drove hoist-gearbox and rope-drum stresses above their fatigue-safe range. An off-vertical pick and slow brake response contributed.
