Condition monitoring captured air temperature 303.8 K, process temperature 314.2 K, rotational speed 2206 rpm, torque 20.9 Nm and accumulated tool wear 85 minutes at the moment of the trip. The shift crew isolated the equipment under the plant permit-to-work system and raised this work order for investigation and repair.

Contributing factors included clogged cooling passages and degraded coolant.

## 4. Corrective Work Completed

Parts and consumables used were booked to this work order, and post-repair verification confirmed the equipment returned to its qualified operating envelope before release back to production.
