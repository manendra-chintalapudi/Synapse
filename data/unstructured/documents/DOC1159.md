Condition monitoring captured air temperature 299.0 K, process temperature 307.8 K, rotational speed 2774 rpm, torque 7.2 Nm and accumulated tool wear 173 minutes at the moment of the trip. The shift crew isolated the equipment under the plant permit-to-work system and raised this work order for investigation and repair.

Contributing factors included clogged cooling passages and degraded coolant.

This finding is the actionable link between the maintenance routine and the failure mechanism, and it was logged so recurrence checks can compare like-for-like across the fleet.

## 4. Corrective Work Completed
