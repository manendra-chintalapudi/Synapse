Questions

Everything teams ask before they try it.

What data does the demo run on?+
A hybrid, provenance-labelled demo: a locked synthetic Rajendra Steel Plant ontology is enriched with the real-source Steel Plates Faults dataset (1,941 rows) and Steel Industry Energy Consumption dataset (35,040 rows). AI4I 2020 adds 10,000 official UCI synthetic-reference records for predictive-maintenance analysis. The sources remain distinguishable across Neo4j, four DuckDB system databases and Chroma.
How are answers cited?+
Every claim carries an inline tag — [Graph:] for Neo4j evidence, [DFS:] for federated SQL results, [RAG:] for document passages — so you can jump from a sentence to the exact node, row or paragraph behind it.
What happens when evidence is missing?+
Synapse says so. Unresolvable questions short-circuit to a clarification instead of an invented answer, and confidence levels are attached to what it does assert.
Is this live or a video?+
Live. The console runs a tiered router, fans out to all three engines in parallel and synthesizes a cited answer in seconds — hit "Launch console" and ask it anything.
